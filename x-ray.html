Upload a X-ray Photo & Adjust the Brightness

*Click and choose file to upload a X-ray photo. Then, the photo will appear in the preview area.


Use the slider to adjust brightness:

Slide left = darker (0%)

Slide right = brighter (200%)

Set slider to 100% to restore original brightness.



Good brightness ensures the image isn't too dark or washed out,

while sufficient contrast allows the eye to distinguish between different elements.
